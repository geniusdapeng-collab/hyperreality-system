# HAVS 项目愿景

## 核心洞察

AI视频（或AI漫剧）领域的真正制高点，只有两个——**剧本**，以及**拍摄脚本**。

剧本决定故事内核，但拍摄脚本的本质，不是传统意义上"给人看"的分镜稿，而是一套**"给模型看"的、适配模型语言的多维度字段化提示词系统**。它涉及几十个维度的参数定义、结构化封装与模型调用适配，是创意到成片之间的工程化桥梁。

把剧本和配套提示词打包交付，用户买完后只需做定妆照、微调台词，就能直接提交渲染、进入分发。最核心的创作门槛被他们解决，剩下的全是低门槛执行工作。

这更加坚定了我的判断：**面向AI视频生成的提示词工程，才是这个领域真正的系统工程**。剧本可以靠创意灵感解决，但拍摄脚本的生成——从创意意图到几十维度的字段化提示词转化，再到系统稳定性与多模型适配——是一个复杂的工程化闭环，缺一不可。

> **剧本是灵魂，运镜是骨架，真实感是底线。**

## English Version

### Core Insight

In the AI video (or AI comic drama) space, there are only two true strategic high grounds — **the script**, and **the shooting script**.

The script defines the story's core, but the essence of a shooting script is not a traditional storyboard meant "for human eyes." Rather, it is a **multi-dimensional, field-based prompt system "for the model to read"** — engineered to speak the model's language. It spans dozens of dimensional parameters, structured encapsulation, and model-call adaptation, serving as the engineering bridge between creative intent and final output.

Once a user buys in, all that remains is preparing reference images, fine-tuning dialogue, and submitting directly for rendering and distribution. The highest creative barriers are solved by them; everything left is low-barrier execution.

This further solidifies my conviction: **prompt engineering for AI video generation is the true systems engineering of this field**. A script can be cracked through creative inspiration, but generating a shooting script — translating creative intent into dozens of dimensional field-based prompts, then ensuring system stability and multi-model adaptation — is a complex engineering closed loop where every component is indispensable.

### System Introduction

**Hyperreal AI Video System (HAVS)** — a hyperreal industrial AI video production system.

At its core, HAVS is about **"Precise Control"** — specifically:
- **Why we control**: from requirements to script
- **How we control**: from professional shot design to 25-dimensional professional video model prompt generation

Only by controlling content with the precision of a director can we deliver usable, high-quality work; otherwise, it's all just toys. We deconstruct camera-movement grammar from classic film industry standards, fusing Harness architecture, Multi-Agent collaboration, and film & television domain skills into a systematic shot-language engineering framework.

> **The script is the soul, the camera work is the skeleton, and photorealism is the baseline.**

### Slogan

**"Harnessing Imagination."**

### Usage

Using HAVS, you simply input a theme, story, or script:
1. The system runs for 10–20 minutes — the speed inherently requires many rounds of deep reasoning, so it takes time
2. Automatically produces a complete rendering script (prompts) for an entire episode, ready to interface directly with the rendering engine
3. Users need only manually refine on top of it (e.g., adjusting character dialogue), or skip edits entirely and submit with one click

**Close the biggest gap between "creative idea" and "finished film."**
